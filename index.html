Ivan Heibi's website

Ivan Heibi is currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Classical Philology and Italian Studies (FICLIT), Bologna, Italy. He is an active member of the Digital Humanities Advanced Research Centre (DHARC), and the Research Centre for Open Scholarly Metadata.

His studies are mainly focused on the integration of the semantic web technologies in the Science Of Science (SoS) field of study, especially toward the Arts and Humanities domain.
